Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Here are some of the questions that are most commonly asked before getting a website started.

Do I need to prepare everything first?

Not at all. It helps if you already have your business name, contact details, and a rough idea of the kind of website you want, but the details can still be shaped along the way as the final version comes together.

How long will it take?

Basic websites are usually completed in about a week. Standard and Premium projects can take up to around three weeks, depending on the amount of content and revisions needed, but the work is always done as efficiently as possible.

Can you update the site later?

Yes. Future changes can still be made, and they would normally count as part of your revision rounds or be arranged as an additional update if needed later on.

What kind of clients is this for?

Freelancers, tutors, creators, service businesses, home-based brands, and anyone who needs a clean online presence.
